Summary

The visualizations in this report demonstrate the effectiveness of reinforcement learning for digital advertising optimization. Key components include:

  • Epsilon-greedy exploration strategy that balances exploration and exploitation
  • A reward function design optimized for ROAS with opportunity cost consideration
  • Experience replay and target networks that stabilize training
  • A risk-managed budget allocation strategy
  • Decision boundaries that incorporate both ROAS and engagement metrics

Together, these components create a robust system for maximizing advertising effectiveness while managing budgets efficiently.
